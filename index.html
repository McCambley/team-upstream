My Experience

Professional

Founder & CEO

Global Zoo

Global Zoo is a grassroots campaign to renaturalize the world.

Since 2004 we've funded the rewilding of over 30,000 acres of rainforest by bringing natures wonders right to the comfort of your home.

By placing discrete cameras in forests across the globe, Global Zoo has become the largest Wildlife Content Creator on the planet.

Our hope is that by bring the world to you, we can educate humanity on the wonders of the Natural World, and ensure that it lives on for future generations.
